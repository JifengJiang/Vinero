About Us

G’est Film is to create commercial videos for Media and ads purposes, with beliefs of top-quality workpiece, passion for innovation, and satisfactory customer experience. G’est Film Production is known for technological improvements and creative designs. We focus on creating the best quality videos for our clients, including company promotion videos, TV commercials, music videos, real-time recording, personal films, and reality shows, etc. Since established, G’EST is always on the leading way and keeps making progress in VFX (highly regarded by industry professionals), Program Packaging, 3D Animation, Corporate promotional videos, and Multi-media designs.
